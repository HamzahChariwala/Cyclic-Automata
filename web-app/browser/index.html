CYCLIC CELLULAR AUTOMATA
Welcome to my cyclic cellular automata simulator. On the right, please fill in the various fields and refresh the page to initiate the program. Following this, use the update button whenever you wish to alter any of the paramaters. This particular program is intended to allow for experimentation with random neighbourhoods, something seemingly unique from my research. For a first run of the software, I would recommend a 50x90 grid with 15 state, a threshold of one 1 and a van-neumann neighbourhood, range 1. Have fun! - Hamzah
